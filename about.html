ABOUT OUR JOB BOARD

A job portal is a website where people who need jobs can find jobs and companies
looking for job seekers can find the perfect employees. It serves as a medium where
job seekers can create profiles, upload resumes, and search for job openings across various industries
and locations. Employers, on the other hand, can post job vacancies, review applicant profiles, and conduct
the initial stages of the hiring process through the portal.

Internship job portals are websites where students and recent graduates can find short-term work experiences to learn
and practice what they’ve studied. These experiences are called internships. These websites connect students with
companies that offer these learning opportunities. It’s like a special place to find temporary jobs that help you learn and
prepare for your future career.
